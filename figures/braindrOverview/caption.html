An overview of our procedure. First, the HBN data set was rated by 4 neuroimaging experts to create a "gold standard" subset of data. Next, the 3D MRI scans were converted into 2D axial brain slices, which were loaded onto braindr (https://braindr.us), a web application to crowdsource the quality ratings.
